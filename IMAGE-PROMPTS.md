## 4. Common Mistakes That Cause Failure

An infographic showing 5 common mistakes in a vertical list with icons.

Each row has an icon, a bold title, and a short explanation:

1. "Overtrading" — Trying to hit the target too fast, taking low-probability setups
2. "Ignoring Daily Drawdown" — One bad day ends the challenge, even if overall P&L is fine
3. "Wrong Position Sizing" — Risking too much per trade relative to daily drawdown limits
4. "Chasing Losses" — Doubling down after a losing trade to recover fast
5. "Unfamiliar Rules" — Not reading the fine print on consistency rules or trailing drawdown

Title: "5 REASONS TRADERS FAIL PROP FIRM CHALLENGES". Dark background. Warning-style design with amber/red accents. 1200x1000px.
