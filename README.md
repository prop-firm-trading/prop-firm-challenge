A prop firm challenge is a paid evaluation. You prove
you can trade profitably within a set of rules. If you
pass, the firm gives you a funded account to trade with
their capital.

That is the simple version. The reality is more nuanced.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  HOW CHALLENGES WORK

  Most prop firms use a two-phase model:

  Phase 1 - Reach a profit target (usually 8-10%)
             within a time limit or no time limit.
             Stay within drawdown limits.

  Phase 2 - Reach a smaller profit target (5-8%).
             Same drawdown rules.

  Funded   - You get a live account. You keep 80-95%
             of profits. Firm takes the rest.

  Some firms offer one-phase challenges. Some offer
  instant funding (skip the evaluation and pay a
  premium). Each model has trade-offs.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  THE KEY RULES TO UNDERSTAND

  1. MAX DRAWDOWN

  This is the total amount you can lose. Usually 8-12%
  of starting balance. If you hit it, you fail.

  Static drawdown: Fixed at starting balance. Easier.
  Trailing drawdown: Moves up with your profits. Harder.

  2. DAILY DRAWDOWN

  How much you can lose in a single day. Usually 4-5%.
  This is the rule that catches most traders. A single
  bad day can end your challenge.

  3. PROFIT TARGET

  How much you need to make. Usually 8-10%. Lower is
  better. Some firms offer 6-8% targets which are
  significantly easier to hit.

  4. TIME LIMIT

  Some firms give you 30 days per phase. Others have
  no time limit. No time limit is easier but can lead
  to overtrading as you try to end the phase fast.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  COMMON TRAPS

  - Consistency rules that force you to hit profit
    targets in a specific way. Avoid these firms.

  - Trailing drawdown that locks in losses. Hard to
    recover from.

  - Hidden fees on payout requests.

  - Payout delays. Check Trustpilot before buying.

  WHICH CHALLENGE TO PICK

  First time? Go with FundedNext or E8 Funding.
  Low cost, low targets, no time limit.

  Experienced? FTMO is the gold standard.
  Higher fees but proven payouts.

  Futures trader? Apex Trader Funding.
  100% profit split on first $10k.
